💡 Tips for best results:
• Use Chrome or Edge for best voice recognition
• Find a quiet spot — background noise affects the microphone
• Hold Space to talk, release when done
• Speak naturally — you can ask for clarification just like a real interview
🎙️ One-time microphone setup (Chrome):
If Chrome keeps asking for mic permission, fix it permanently in seconds:
1. Click the icon at the left of the address bar (may look like 🔒 or ⊙ or two small dots)
2. Click "Site settings"
3. Set Microphone → Allow
You only need to do this once.
